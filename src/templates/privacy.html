Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and password. We may also collect information about your pets that you choose to provide while using our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to provide and improve our pet care services. Your email is used for account login and important updates. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are encrypted before being saved.

4. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in while you use the app. These cookies are deleted when you log out or close your browser.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time. If you would like your data removed, please contact us.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@petcarea.com.

This privacy policy applies to the PetCareA web application.
